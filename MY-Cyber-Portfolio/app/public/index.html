My academic journey includes advanced certifications from South Metropolitan TAFE Murdoch, where I completed the 22334VIC Certificate IV and the 22445VIC Advanced Diploma in Cyber Security. These programs have equipped me with a deep understanding of network security, threat analysis, and the legal frameworks surrounding digital security.
